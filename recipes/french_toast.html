French Toast

Description

This simple French toast recipe soaks thick slices of bread in a rich egg and milk mixture to make golden, fluffy slices for a delicious breakfast treat. Works with white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French bread! Delicious served hot with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more as needed

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl until combined and smooth.
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat. Soak each slice of bread in egg mixture, about 5 to 10 seconds per side, allowing it to absorb the mixture without becoming soggy.
  4. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden brown and slightly crisp, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy with butter and syrup.
